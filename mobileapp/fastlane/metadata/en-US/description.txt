gtmux is a radar and command center for the agents running in tmux on your Mac — coding agents like Claude and Codex, or any agent that lives in a terminal. Run gtmux serve on your Mac, pair your phone once, and take the whole fleet with you.

SEE
A live radar of every tmux pane and CLI agent, color-coded by state — waiting for you, working, or idle. One glance tells you who needs you.

STEER
Reply, or send a command straight to a terminal, from your phone. Pull an agent into focus. Unblock work without walking back to your Mac.

SUPERVISE
An HQ that keeps watch for you: a supervisor digests what every agent is doing, surfaces only what actually needs your attention, and can triage and hand off work — so a busy fleet stays quiet until it matters.

ALSO
• Push the moment an agent needs you or finishes — not before.
• Live Activity — the current agent's state on your Lock Screen and Dynamic Island.
• Share, safely — a scoped, revocable link lets a teammate see only the sessions you allow and type only into the panes you allow. Input is off by default.
• English and 简体中文.

HOW IT WORKS
gtmux talks to a small server (gtmux serve) on your own Mac, over your own network, VPN, or tunnel — like an SSH or terminal client for your own machine. Nothing is uploaded to us, there is no account, and no code runs on your phone. Access is gated by a token you control and can revoke anytime.

PRIVACY
gtmux collects no data. Your sessions and tokens stay on your device and your Mac.
